Carrot cake Recipe

Description

Rich and flavorful cake made from carrot and flour, it's nice and tender to the mouth and test is guaranteed. check below for Ingredients and steps to make.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • oil/butter
  • eggs
  • baking powder
  • blended carrots

steps

  1. break and beat your eggs
  2. add eggs to flour and baking powder
  3. add your blended carrot
  4. mix together using your mixing machine
